AREA NOTATION
--Chapter, area, and map notation
  -Example 1: 1-4(Central Labs)(5)-3(Lobby)(2)
    -First number: chapter number
    -Second number: area number (global)
    -Third number: room number (within area)

    -Words in parenthasies: name of area (optional)
    -Numbers in parenthasies: version of area/room


DEFINITIONS
  -Room
    -A map made in [tiles].
    -Can function as a room in a building, an area outside like a sidewalk in between buildings, a group of rooms, etc.
  -Area
    -A group of rooms arranged by physical location within the universe used for design and programming organization
